**Player Fantasy**
> Attendant of the fable environment 

**Core Gameplay Loop**
> 1. Observe - You get informed of your current problem
> 2. Roaming - You are presented the tools/resources you will need to solve it. 
> 3. Create/Mutate - With the given tools, you create your first life form 
> 4. Release - Set the creature out to the world, the completion of the problem

**Progression System**
> Observing the interactions and results of the player made changes.
> Getting access to new environments (plants, animals, genes)

**Key Systems/Mechanics**
> Creation of mythical flora & fauna
> Individual traits of the interactable creatures (touch rocks)

**Unique Selling Point (USP)**
> 1. Emergent gameplay & Story
> 2. Calming 
> 3. Extremely smooth player progression system (THE METH THE CRACK THE GRATIFICATION)

**Design Pillars**
> 1. A simple start - one job at a time, a first problem solved. 
> 2. Complications should arise from player completion or later on result of solved problems or due to problem solving. 
> 3. Learning new mechanics not due to tutorials but during the emergent problem solving.
